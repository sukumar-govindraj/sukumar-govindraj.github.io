Agentic systems philosophy

From models to operational agents.

I’m interested in AI systems that do more than generate text. Useful enterprise agents understand business context, structure messy inputs, apply rules, use tools, and produce auditable outputs.

01

Design around the workflow

Start from the business process, approval points, exception paths, and downstream actions — not from a model demo.

02

Make outputs structured

Prefer JSON, tables, process maps, tickets, dashboards, and action-ready records over loose text.

03

Keep humans in the loop

Use human review where judgment, compliance, or financial impact requires approval.

04

Measure operational value

Evaluate time saved, risk reduced, error reduction, adoption, and decision quality — not just model novelty.
